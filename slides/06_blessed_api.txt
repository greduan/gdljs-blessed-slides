% Some facts

* Blessed uses factories for creating its elements.  Thus your program probably
  should as well.

* Every element can have a ~style~ property to control how it looks.

* Every element has a ~_~, ~$~ or ~data~ property to store some of your
  program's variables.

* You can access and manipulate every element's ~width~, ~height~, ~top~ and
  ~left~ properties, allowing you to manipulate its position on the screen.

* You can manipulate an element's ~width~, ~height~ and position in relation to
  its parent node or in relation to the screen.
